About

Where is it located?

Guntur is a city and the administrative headquarters of Guntur district in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Guntur is a central part of the state and city is spread across 198.7 km square and is the third-largest city in the state.

What is famous here?

The district of Guntur is particularly famous for its red-hot chillies. The Guntur chilli is popular for its pungency and witnesses high demand from other countries.

When is the best time to visit?

Winters in the area are mildly cool and pleasant enough to explore its hidden treasures. Thus, the time period between October and March is recommended as the ideal time to visit Guntur.

Things To Do in Guntur District
